ABOUT GDGC
Google Developer Groups on Campus (GDGC) are university-based communities of students interested in Google developer technologies. 
Students from any undergraduate or postgraduate major are welcome to join the GDG, from new developers to experienced ones.
 GDGC enables students to learn from fellow students and work on projects to have practical exposure.

WHO CAN JOIN

Any student, including beginners, intermediate learners, and advanced developers who wish to explore modern tools and grow in technology.
OPPORTUNITIES OFFERED BY GDGC
- Peer-to-peer workshops and events
Real-world problem solving for local organizations

- Showing prototypes to industry experts

Inspiring stories from experienced changemakers who are developers.

VISION
A community of passionate student developers learning, building, and growing through solving real-world problems using technology.

MISSION
Provide resources, mentorship, and opportunities that help students grow as developers and build innovative tech solutions.
